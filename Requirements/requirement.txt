---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PACKAGE               | PURPOSE / USAGE
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
torch                 | Core deep learning library (PyTorch). GPU/CPU tensor ops & model training.
torchvision           | Vision add-on for PyTorch. Pretrained CNNs, image transforms, datasets.
torchaudio            | Audio add-on for PyTorch. Load/process waveforms, speech/audio models.
transformers          | Hugging Face library for NLP & LLMs (BERT, GPT, LLaMA, etc.).
sentence-transformers | High-level embeddings on top of Transformers for similarity/search.
faiss-cpu             | Facebook AI Similarity Search (CPU). Fast vector search & clustering.
pandas                | DataFrames for structured data. CSV/Excel/SQL read-write, analysis.
numpy                 | Base math array library. Fast matrix ops for ML & data processing.
tqdm                  | Progress bars for loops, downloads, or training epochs.
psutil                | Monitor CPU, RAM, and process stats during ML or system tasks.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
